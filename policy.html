3. Data Collection

We collect the following personal data:

  • Email Address: Collected when you sign in via Google API to access the Web App.

We do not collect sensitive personal data (e.g., financial, health, biometric information).

Additionally, we maintain a manual list of authorized users (including email addresses) for purposes of managing access to the Web App. This list originates from individuals voluntarily providing their email to designees of the Group and is used to verify user access. While this list is separate from the Web App's functionality, the Web App may reference it to validate authorized users.

Please remember that this policy covers only data collected through the Web App, not all data collected or used by the Group.

4. Purpose of Data Collection

We use your email address for:

  • Authorization: To verify your identity and confirm that you are authorized to access and use the Web App.
  • Session Management: Temporarily storing your email to enable actions without requiring repeated logins.

The manual list of authorized users may also be shared with other individuals within the Group to facilitate coordination and communication among members.

5. Legal Basis for Data Processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Legitimate Interest (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR): Ensuring authorized access to the Web App and maintaining session continuity.
  • Performance of a Contract (Article 6(1)(b) GDPR): Allowing users to interact with the Web App as part of its core functionality.

6. Data Retention

  • Temporary Storage: Your email address is temporarily stored in the back-end Google Apps Script project's cache. This data is automatically deleted no later than 6 hours after your session ends.
  • Local Storage: The Web App uses local storage/cache to store:
    • Your email address (for session continuity).
    • An authorization token to validate requests to the back-end.

We do not store any personal data within the Web App permanently.

7. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or disclose your personal data to third parties outside the group of individuals. However:

  • The manual list of authorized users (including email addresses) may be shared within the private Group for purposes of coordination and communication.
